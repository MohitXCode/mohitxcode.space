## The fear

Putting unfinished work out there is scary. People might judge. They might see you fail. They might think you are not good enough.

## The reality

No one is watching that closely. Everyone is too busy with their own work. The people who do watch are usually supportive — or learning alongside you.

## The benefit

Accountability. Feedback. Connections. A portfolio that builds itself. When you learn in public, your work becomes your resume.

## Just start

You do not need permission. You do not need to be an expert. Share what you are learning, as you learn it. The compound effect is real.
