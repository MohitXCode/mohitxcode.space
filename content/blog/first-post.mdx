## Why build from scratch?

I could have used a template. I could have copied a theme. But I wanted to understand every line of code that runs this site. Building from scratch forces you to make decisions — and live with them.

## The stack

I chose Next.js 15 with the App Router. TypeScript for safety. Tailwind CSS for speed. Framer Motion for delight. No UI library — every component is custom.

## The hardest part

Not the code. The design decisions. What to include, what to cut, when to stop polishing. Perfection is the enemy of shipped.

## What's next

More projects. More writing. More learning in public. This is just the beginning.
